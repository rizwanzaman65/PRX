WELCOME TO PRX RESEARCH

PRX Research is committed to accelerating the translation of biomedical discoveries into breakthrough healthcare solutions. Our team is here to collaborate on your breakthrough and leverage our expertise to turn research into reality.

PRX Research is the research division of a 202-bed hospital designed to meet the growing medical needs of northeast Texas residents. It offers a comprehensive range of health services for acute and emergencies, including 24-hour Emergency Room; critical care; medical, surgical, stroke, and cardiac services including open heart surgery; outpatient surgery; orthopedics; and imaging services.
